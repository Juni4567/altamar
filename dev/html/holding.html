Welcome to the Altamar Global Politics Podcast

We're pleased to be launching this podcast, hosted by political analyst Peter Schechter and journalist Muni Jensen, which will explore crucial international affairs issues in these turbulent times. Altamar, or "high seas," aims to cut through the noise and confusion of the deepening polarization of the moment, and deliver an honest debate to find our way back to shore.
